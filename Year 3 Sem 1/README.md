# Year 3 — Semester 1

**Advanced systems, artificial intelligence, enterprise design, and interdisciplinary electives.**

</div>

---

The penultimate semester combines advanced technical coursework with workplace-based application. Artificial intelligence, advanced database programming, and enterprise-level systems design are complemented by interdisciplinary electives that broaden the graduate profile.

---

## Courses

### 🤖 [Artificial Intelligence](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%201/Artificial%20Intelligence)
**AI concepts, search algorithms, and introductory machine learning.**

Covers search strategies (BFS, DFS, A*), knowledge representation, inference engines, and foundational machine learning concepts. Provides the theoretical grounding for understanding and applying AI techniques within data engineering pipelines.

---

### 🗃️ [Database Programming](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%201/Database%20Programming)
**Advanced database development and application integration.**

Extends database knowledge into stored procedures, triggers, views, transaction control, query optimisation, and the development of fully functional database-driven applications. Builds directly on the relational database foundations of Year 2.

---

### 🏢 [Enterprise System Design and Modelling (WBL)](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%201/Enterprise%20System%20Design%20and%20Modelling%20(WBL))
**Enterprise architecture modelling in a workplace-based learning context.**

Applies enterprise architecture frameworks and modelling techniques — including business process modelling and system integration design — to real organisational environments. Develops the ability to bridge technical design and business requirements at scale.

---

### 🔭 [Field Astronomy](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%201/Field%20Astronomy)
**Observational astronomy and celestial measurement.**

Covers celestial coordinate systems, star catalogue usage, telescope operation, and precision field measurement techniques. Develops rigorous observational methodology and attention to data accuracy in a scientific fieldwork context.

---

### 🇫🇷 [French Language](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%201/French%20Language)
**Introductory French language for communication.**

Develops foundational French language skills in vocabulary, grammar, and everyday conversation. Supports cross-cultural communication competency relevant to international professional and academic environments.

---

### 🎤 [Professional Communication Skill 2](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%201/Professional%20Communication%20Skill%202)
**Advanced professional communication and stakeholder engagement.**

Builds on earlier communication coursework with a focus on persuasive presentation, technical reporting, stakeholder communication, and professional negotiation — skills directly applicable in industry and research settings.
