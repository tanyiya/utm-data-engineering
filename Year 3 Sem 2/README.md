# Year 3 — Semester 2

**Application development, business intelligence, high-performance data processing, and innovation.**

</div>

---

The final semester brings together the full scope of the programme into production-oriented, large-scale coursework. From deploying end-to-end applications to processing data at distributed scale, this semester represents the applied culmination of three years of technical development.

---

## Courses

### 📱 [Application Development](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%202/Application%20Development)
**End-to-end development and deployment of software applications.**

Covers front-end and back-end integration, RESTful API design, authentication, and deployment pipelines. Develops the capability to architect and deliver complete, production-ready applications across web and mobile platforms.

---

### 📊 [Business Intelligence](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%202/Business%20Intelligence)
**Data warehousing, ETL pipelines, and BI reporting.**

Explores data warehouse architecture, dimensional modelling (star and snowflake schemas), ETL design, OLAP analysis, and dashboarding with tools such as Power BI or Tableau. Bridges analytical engineering and executive-level decision support.

---

### 🚀 [Entrepreneurship and Innovation](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%202/Entrepreneurship%20and%20Innovation)
**Venture creation, innovation methodology, and business model design.**

Applies design thinking, lean startup frameworks, and business model canvas methodology to the development of technology-driven ventures. Develops the strategic and commercial thinking that complements technical expertise.

---

### ⚡ [High Performance Data Processing](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%202/High%20Performance%20Data%20Processing)
**Distributed computing and large-scale data processing.**

Covers parallel processing architectures, the MapReduce paradigm, Apache Spark, and stream processing frameworks. Develops the skills to design and implement data pipelines capable of operating efficiently at enterprise and big data scale.

---

### 🔬 [Special Topic in Data Engineering](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%203%20Sem%202/Special%20Topic%20in%20Data%20Engineering)
**Advanced or emerging topic in data engineering.**

A focused study of a current frontier area in data engineering — such as data lakehouses, real-time streaming architectures, MLOps, or vector databases. Designed to engage with the evolving demands of the data engineering profession.
