# 📊 Course Reflection: Business Intelligence (BI)

The *Business Intelligence* course offered an advanced, hands-on dive into the architecture, deployment, and execution of data-driven decision systems. The curriculum focused on the foundational pillars of enterprise BI, encompassing data warehousing paradigms, Extract-Transform-Load (ETL) engineering, advanced semantic data modeling, and dashboard design. The technical stack focused heavily on utilizing **Alteryx** for automated, complex data orchestration and **Microsoft Power BI** for deep interactive visual analytics.

### 🏛️ Capstone BI Project: Malaysian Banking Sector Analysis
The primary execution of the course involved an end-to-end business intelligence project investigating the financial performance of major Malaysian banking institutions (including Maybank, CIMB, and RHB). The analysis integrated internal corporate financial metrics with critical macroeconomic indicators—such as Gross Domestic Product (GDP), the Overnight Policy Rate (OPR), and the U.S. Federal Funds Rate—to evaluate external economic influences on market equity performance.
