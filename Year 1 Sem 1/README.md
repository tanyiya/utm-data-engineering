# Year 1 — Semester 1

**Foundational studies in computing, mathematics, and programming.**

</div>

---

This semester establishes the core technical and intellectual foundations of the programme. Coursework spans digital systems, mathematical reasoning, programming fundamentals, and the ethical responsibilities of computing professionals.

---

## Courses

### 🔌 [Digital Logic](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%201%20Sem%201/Digital%20Logic)
**Introduction to digital circuits and binary systems.**

Covers boolean algebra, logic gates, combinational and sequential circuit design, Karnaugh maps, and flip-flops. Provides the hardware-level understanding that underpins all computing systems.

---

### 🧮 [Discrete Structure](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%201%20Sem%201/Discrete%20Structure)
**Mathematical foundations of computer science.**

Explores sets, relations, functions, propositional and predicate logic, proof techniques, graph theory, trees, and combinatorics — the formal mathematical framework that appears throughout algorithms, data structures, and theoretical computing.

---

### 🤝 [Integrity and Anti-Corruption Course](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%201%20Sem%201/Integrity%20and%20Anti%20Corruption%20Course)
**Ethics and professional responsibility in computing.**

Examines principles of integrity, transparency, and anti-corruption practices within professional and public service contexts. Develops awareness of ethical obligations relevant to technology practitioners.

---

### 💻 [Programming Technique](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%201%20Sem%201/Programming%20Technique)
**Core programming logic and problem solving in C/C++.**

Introduces structured programming concepts including variables, control flow, functions, arrays, and basic problem decomposition. Establishes the programming discipline carried forward throughout the degree.

---

### 🖥️ [Technology and Information System](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%201%20Sem%201/Technology%20and%20Information%20System)
**Overview of IT infrastructure and organisational information systems.**

Surveys hardware, software, networking, and data management concepts, and examines how information systems support decision-making and operations in modern organisations.
