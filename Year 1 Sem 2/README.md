# Year 1 — Semester 2

This directory contains course folders for Year 1 Semester 2. Click a course to open its folder.

- **[Appreciation of Ethics and Civilizations](Appreciation%20of%20Ethics%20and%20Civilizations/):** Study of ethical frameworks, cultural development, and civilizational impacts on society.
- **[Computer Organisation and Architecture](Computer%20Organisation%20and%20Architecture/):** Concepts of computer hardware, instruction sets, CPU design, and assembly language labs.
- **[Human Computer Interaction](Human%20Computer%20Interaction/):** Principles of user-centred design, usability, and interface evaluation.
- **[Probability & Statistical Data Analysis](Probability%20&%20Statistical%20Data%20Analysis/):** Introductory probability theory and statistical methods for data analysis.
- **[Programming Technique II](Programming%20Technique%20II/):** Continued programming concepts, structured programming, and intermediate C/C++ projects.
- **[System Analysis and Design (WBL)](System%20Analysis%20and%20Design%20(WBL)/):** Practical systems analysis and design methodologies in a workplace-based learning context.
