# Year 1 — Semester 2

This directory contains course folders for Year 1 Semester 2. Click a course to open its folder.

- **[Appreciation of Ethics and Civilizations](Appreciation of Ethics and Civilizations/):** Study of ethical frameworks, cultural development, and civilizational impacts on society.
- **[Computer Organisation and Architecture](Computer Organisation and Architecture/):** Concepts of computer hardware, instruction sets, CPU design, and assembly language labs.
- **[Human Computer Interaction](Human Computer Interaction/):** Principles of user-centred design, usability, and interface evaluation.
- **[Probability & Statistical Data Analysis](Probability & Statistical Data Analysis/):** Introductory probability theory and statistical methods for data analysis.
- **[Programming Technique II](Programming Technique II/):** Continued programming concepts, structured programming, and intermediate C/C++ projects.
- **[System Analysis and Design (WBL)](System Analysis and Design (WBL)/):** Practical systems analysis and design methodologies in a workplace-based learning context.
