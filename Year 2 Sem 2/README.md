# Year 2 — Semester 2

**Data analytics, object-oriented programming, operating systems, and professional development.**

</div>

---

This semester marks a clear pivot toward data-oriented computing. Coursework introduces data analytics programming, data mining techniques, and object-oriented design — while also building the professional communication and entrepreneurial awareness expected of industry-ready graduates.

---

## Courses

### 📈 [Data Analytics Programming](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%202%20Sem%202/Data%20Analytics%20Programming)
**Programming-driven data analysis using Python.**

Covers data manipulation, cleaning, and visualisation using Python libraries including Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib. Applies analytical programming to real-world datasets, building practical fluency in the primary language of data science.

---

### 🔍 [Data Mining](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%202%20Sem%202/Data%20Mining)
**Pattern discovery and knowledge extraction from large datasets.**

Studies classification, clustering, association rule mining, regression, and model evaluation methods. Applies techniques using tools such as Weka and scikit-learn, developing the ability to extract actionable insights from complex data.

---

### 🧱 [Object Oriented Programming](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%202%20Sem%202/Object%20Oriented%20Programming)
**Object-oriented design principles and implementation.**

Covers classes, objects, encapsulation, inheritance, polymorphism, and abstraction in Java or Python. Develops the structured design thinking required for building maintainable, scalable software systems.

---

### 🖥️ [Operating System](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%202%20Sem%202/Operating%20System)
**Operating system concepts, process management, and resource allocation.**

Examines process scheduling, memory management (paging and segmentation), file systems, synchronisation, and I/O management. Provides a thorough understanding of the system layer beneath every application.

---

### 🌍 [Philosophy and Current Issue](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%202%20Sem%202/Philosophy%20and%20Current%20Issue)
**Philosophical inquiry into contemporary global and technological issues.**

Engages with current societal challenges — including digital ethics, sustainability, and inequality — through structured philosophical reasoning. Develops critical thinking and the ability to form well-argued positions on complex issues.

---

### 🗣️ [Professional Communication Skill](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%202%20Sem%202/Professional%20Communication%20Skill)
**Technical writing, presentation, and professional communication.**

Develops written and oral communication skills for professional contexts, including structured report writing, technical presentations, and effective communication with both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

---

### 💡 [Technopreneurship Seminar](https://github.com/tanyiya/utm-data-engineering/blob/main/Year%202%20Sem%202/Technopreneurship%20Seminar)
**Technology entrepreneurship, startup culture, and innovation frameworks.**

Explores the intersection of technology and entrepreneurship through seminars, case studies, and industry engagement. Covers business model design, MVP development, and the mindset required to build technology ventures.
